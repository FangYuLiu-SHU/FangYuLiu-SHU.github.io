Biography

Weimin Li, Male, Doctor of Engineering, is currently a professor at the School of Computer Engineering and Science of Shanghai University and a visiting researcher at Waseda University, Japan. He has been working at Shanghai University since April 2008. In 2013, he was funded by the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS) to visit Waseda University in Japan for two months. He also had visited the University of California, Santa Barbara from December 2015 to December 2016. He is the director of Shanghai Computer Users Association and Supervisor of Shanghai Computer Open Systems Association. He has published more than 50 academic papers in international journals, IEEE international conferences and domestic core journals.

Research Interests

Big Data Analysis、Social Network Analysis、Bioinformatics、Service Calculation、Database Technology、Business Process Management、Intelligent Information Processing, etc.
